PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, business address proof (electricity bill or rent agreement), bank account details, and one passport-size photograph. For companies, MOA/AOA and Certificate of Incorporation are also required. We guide you through every step and handle the entire portal filing on your behalf.
Every individual with annual income above ₹2.5 lakh (basic exemption limit) must file ITR. It is also mandatory for anyone claiming a tax refund, applying for a visa, taking a bank loan, or carrying forward capital losses. Even with no tax liability, filing ITR builds strong financial credibility.
GSTR-3B is due on the 20th of every month for regular monthly filers. For QRMP scheme taxpayers, the due date is the 22nd or 24th of the month following the quarter end (depending on your state). Late filing attracts ₹50/day penalty (₹20/day for nil returns). AmarTax tracks all your deadlines so you never miss one.
Udyam Registration is the official MSME certificate issued by the Government of India. Any manufacturing or service business with investment below ₹50 crore and turnover below ₹250 crore is eligible. Benefits include priority bank loans at lower interest, government subsidies, protection against payment delays beyond 45 days, and eligibility for various government schemes.
Do not ignore it. Most GST notices — DRC-01, DRC-03, scrutiny notices — have a fixed response deadline of 7 to 30 days. Contact AmarTax Advisors immediately. We review the notice, prepare a detailed professional reply with all supporting documents, and represent you before the GST officer. The sooner you act, the better the outcome.
TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) applies when you make payments above threshold limits for salary, professional fees, rent, commission, or contractor payments. The deductor must deposit TDS by the 7th of the following month and file quarterly returns (Form 24Q/26Q). Late deposit attracts 1.5%/month interest. We handle complete TDS compliance so you avoid all penalties.
