PAN Card, Aadhaar Card, business address proof (electricity bill / rent agreement), bank account details, and passport-size photograph. For companies: MOA/AOA and Certificate of Incorporation. We guide you through every step and handle the entire portal filing.
Every individual with annual income above ₹2.5 lakh (basic exemption limit) must file an ITR. It is also mandatory for those claiming tax refunds, for foreign travel, visa applications, or bank loans. Even if you have no tax liability, filing an ITR builds your financial credibility.
GSTR-3B is due on the 20th of every month for regular monthly filers. Under the QRMP scheme, the due date is the 22nd or 24th of the month following quarter end, depending on the state. Late filing attracts ₹50/day penalty (₹20/day for nil returns). AmarTax ensures you never miss these dates.
Udyam Registration is the official MSME certificate from the Government of India. Any manufacturing or service business with investment below ₹50 crore and turnover below ₹250 crore is eligible. Benefits include priority bank loans, subsidized rates, protection against payment delays, and eligibility for various government schemes.

TDS (Tax Deducted at Source) applies when you make payments above threshold limits for salary, professional fees, rent, commission, contractor payments, etc. The deductor must deposit TDS by the 7th of the following month and file quarterly returns (Form 24Q/26Q).
